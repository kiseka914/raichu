Born from lightning

First introduced in 1996, Raichu was brought to life by Atsuko Nishida and finalized by Ken Sugimori. As the evolved form of the iconic Pikachu, it embodies a power that transcends its precursor.

Shocking Potential

100k+ Volts

Raichu's tail acts as a ground, protecting it from its own high-voltage discharges.

Known for its agility in battle, it can outpace almost any opponent when charged.

In the Alola region, Raichu takes on a Psychic/Electric form, using its tail as a surfboard. This unique evolution highlights the adaptability of this incredible species.
